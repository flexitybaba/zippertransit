1
Download the app

Get the Marketplace app now from the Google Play Store or the App Store. Let the app lead you to the user home page where you see lists of professions.

2
Choose any profession

Discover a wide range of professions on the first page of Zipper Transit, including barbers, hairstylists, plumbers, electricians, mechanics, event planners, and more, categorized for easy navigation.

3

How It Works For Pro

1
Download the app

Get the Marketplace Pro app now from the Google Play Store or the App Store. If you have a registered account with us sign in otherwise see step 2.

2

Start by registering your business information on Zipper Transit Marketplace. The process is simple and takes just 3-5 minutes. This quick step sets the foundation for your presence on the platform.

3
Fast and Thorough Review

After submitting your registration, our team will review your information within 24 hours. We ensure that all professionals meet our quality standards before going live.

4
Go Live and Get Hired

Once your registration is approved, your profile goes live automatically. You can start receiving customer inquiries right away. With your services now available to a broad audience.
